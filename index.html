Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transfromation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"This is the time to redesign. The most important step in the redesigning process would be to create options for people. Our present system doesn't offer any options. Its obstinacy and rigidity have taken away the capacity for self-corrections. If we replace it with a system that provides options to everyone regarding all basic economic decisions, a corrective mechanism will come into play and steer the system in the right direction. It is difficult to orient the older generation to get used to having options and making their own decisions because they were never introduced to these options."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
